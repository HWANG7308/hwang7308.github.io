Bio

Hi! I am Hao Wang, currently a Ph.D. student at the Division of Production Systems, Department of Industrial and Materials Science, Chalmers University of Technology. My research interests are in object detection and pose estimation, and their application in robotics and manufacturing. I have background in signal and image processing, embedded system, machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. In 2019, I received my MSc with Distinction in Informatics from The University of Edinburgh. In 2018, I received my B.Eng. in Telecommunication Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.
